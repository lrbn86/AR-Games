About AR Games

AR Games is the site for curated AR (Augmented Reality) games. This site was created to provide the convenience of finding AR games to play.

What is Augmented Reality?

Augmented Reality is an interactive experience of a real-world environment where the objects that reside in the real world are enhanced by computer-generated perceptual information. One of the most well known AR game is called Pokemon Go!, which was released in July 6, 2016.
